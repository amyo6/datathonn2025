Modecraft

An in-depth analysis of Modecraft's retail data, uncovering key growth opportunities and forecasting future operational potential.

Modecraft is a dominant force in the European retail sector, offering a wide range of products such as mugs, cabinets, lanterns, and other home goods. With over half a million items ordered, the company is poised for even greater success. Our team analyzed the data and developed visualizations to highlight strategic opportunities that could drive further growth.

Major Highlights

Through our analysis, we developed a series of visualizations that highlighted Modecraft’s key strengths. We found that the United Kingdom was the region where they achieved the greatest revenue. Overall, the company's total revenue remained steady with consistent positive growth. Additionally, we identified their top-selling items and uncovered valuable insights into customer behavior.

Based on our analysis, we recommend that Modecraft prioritize expanding its presence in the United Kingdom, invest in developing products with higher revenue potential, and strategically boost sales efforts on Thursdays.
